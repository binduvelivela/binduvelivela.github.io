App Name: Explore
Slogan: xplore your interests and follow your passion
Role: Developer
Languages used: Android Studio, Java
About project: this technology driven era social media has taken a huge leap. We can see a uneducated person these days but it has become hard to see a person who is not using any kind of social media. In my opinion, no matter how many social media sites we have new ones are always in demand. So, taking that as a lead this app proposal is made.Just like how Snapchat was initially focused on private, person-to-person photo sharing, but you can now use it for a range of asks, including sending videos, live video chatting, messaging, creating caricature-like Bitmoji avatars, and sharing a chronological “story” that's broadcasted to all your followers. We are also going to focus on sharing the pictures and information to only groups, there won’t be in any individual group. Also, One category can have only one group. If anyone wants to add a new group they should raise a request and the request will be accepted only if that is a non existing group. This is to avoid the loss of information. If there are multiple groups then information will be divided, but people who are interested in that may not join all the groups. It would also be a hassle to follow those many groups. One more feature our website is going to have is one individual can have only one account their ID will be verified at the time of registration. Now a days with the increased social media crimes are also increased, cyberbullying and cybercrimes has become normal. It is not easy to trust neither content nor people, but in our website with our one individual one account. It will also have some of common social media features like chatting and etc., but they will be our secondary focus.
This app is still in progress
