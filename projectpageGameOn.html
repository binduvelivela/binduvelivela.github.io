App Name: Game On
Slogan: Get things done at your convenience
Role: Team member, Business Analyst
Languages used: Android Studio, Java
About project: This is for the user who want to play games but don't know where to go. They can simply install the app and based on their location a list of gaming categories will be displayed. Once he chose the category he can see the list of available gaming courts near him. Gaming court price and other details will also be displayed to the user. He can book the court at the required date and time. Here he can also pay the fees for booking the court.
